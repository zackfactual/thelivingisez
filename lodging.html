Where to Stay

We've reserved a block of rooms at the Country Inn and Suites , near downtown Northfield. Other local lodging includes the Fairfield Inn and Suites. Both are in very walkable locations and are convenient to shuttles to the wedding venue.

Getting to Northfield from MSP International Airport

Northfield is about a 45 minute drive from MSP Airport. Additionally, Northfield Lines bus company runs a shuttle from the airport to downtown Northfield. Tickets cost around $25 each way. You can find their schedule here.
