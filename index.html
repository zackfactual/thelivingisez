Our Story

Elena and Zachary met the old-fashioned way: in a bar. Elena was looking to kill time while waiting for a friend’s delayed flight. Zachary, who’d been in Seattle one week, was working his third shift at a cider house when Elena came in and asked for a recommendation. Zachary gave her a bad one, and it got them talking about biology and French film. They bonded over their shared love of books, nature, and travel, and it wasn’t long before they got engaged in a field of tulips in Skagit Valley. Since then they have traveled to three countries, seen two solar eclipses, adopted a dog, started a charity fund, and driven across the USA. Recently, they bought a house in Minneapolis, near to Elena’s home town, where they will be getting married this summer. They look forward to welcoming family and friends to the festivities.
